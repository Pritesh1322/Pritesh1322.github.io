Lighting Setup

1. Soft and fresh lighting
Somewhat similar to the soft and shadow setup in our three light section, this setup is perfect for small studio spaces (though it does require some modification to the space). For the lighting, three carefully positioned lights shining into the white studio wall on one side of the model combined with a large softbox on the opposite side serve to create a very contoured yet flattering light. Ideal for single or group portraiture, this setup produces a lovely soft, fresh light using just two types of modifiers — it doesn’t get much simpler!
